About Libbie

Libbie is a modern and social web application for book lovers who want more than just a place to track their reading. Whether you're an avid reader, a casual book collector, or someone seeking literary inspiration, Libbie helps you organize your personal digital library, share reviews, and discover your next great read.

Unlike traditional book platforms, Libbie gives you full creative freedom to build and customize your own bookshelves. You can sort books by genre, theme, or any system that fits your unique reading habits. All books are connected to rich metadata and summaries, powered by open book databases, making discovery and organization seamless.

The Team Behind Libbie

Libbie was created by a group of system development students at Malmö University. As part of a 12-week agile software project, the team designed and built Libbie using Java, HTML/CSS, and modern development practices like Scrum, version control, usability testing, and iterative prototyping. The project represents not only a functioning web application, but also a demonstration of collaboration, creativity, and passion for user-centered design.

Let Libbie be your companion in building your personal library—and your literary connections.
